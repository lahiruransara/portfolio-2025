Overview
Worldwise: The Wisdom App
Worldwise: The Wisdom App is a mindfulness and learning experience that brings daily quotes, ideas, and cultural wisdom from around the world, all in one beautifully designed space. The goal was to create an app that feels calm, thoughtful, and globally inclusive, helping users discover, learn, and grow through timeless wisdom.
Design Challenge
The challenge was to design a product that:
  • Feels human and emotionally intelligent not just another quote app.
  • Encourages daily engagement without overwhelming the user.
  • Balances minimalism and cultural diversity in the visual language.
  • Scales gracefully for multilingual and cross-cultural content.
Target Audience
This app is designed for individuals seeking daily inspiration, mindfulness, and cultural insight, people who value reflection and personal growth in a fast-paced world.
My Role
As the product designer for Worldwise, I was responsible for shaping the entire user experience and visual language:
  • Core Experience: Designing the main flows for daily quotes and cultural discovery.
  • Design System: Defining typography, color palette, iconography, and motion for a cohesive interface.
  • Dark Themed UI: Crafting visually appealing dark-themed designs optimized for readability.
  • Widgets & Micro-Interactions: Designing subtle interactions and widgets that encourage daily engagement.
  • Collaboration: Working closely with developers to refine animations, layouts, and accessibility compliance.
Design Process
We explored how users interact with reflective and motivational apps (e.g., Headspace, Stoic, Insight Timer) and found that users seek meaning, not just motivation wisdom with cultural depth and a calm, focused experience.
I designed Worldwise around a simple, personalized, and culturally diverse experience:
  • Culture Selection: Users choose one or more cultures from a large list, and the app surfaces quotes relevant to their selection.
  • Quote Interaction: Users can swipe through quotes on the home page, save quotes they like, or hide ones they dont want to see. Each quote includes its reference for context.
  • Sharing: Users can easily share quotes on social media while keeping author attribution intact.
  • Prototyping & Testing: I created Figma prototypes to validate flows for onboarding, culture selection, browsing, saving/hiding, viewing references, and sharing. Feedback ensured the experience is intuitive and engaging.
  • Accessibility & Scalability: Layouts were optimized to handle long quotes, and interactions meet accessibility standards for tap targets and readability.
Key Features Designed
  • Frequently Updating Widgets: Seamlessly blends into users daily routine.
  • Culture-Based Discovery: Learn the origin, author, and philosophy behind each quote.
  • Personal Library: Save and revisit your favorite quotes.
  • Minimal Animations: Subtle transitions to maintain focus on content.

Conclusion & Developer Handoff
The first version of Worldwise brought to life a calm, culturally rich experience that helps people slow down and reflect each day. For this release, I designed the mobile and tablet UIs, created responsive layouts, and worked on App Store visuals, branding logos, and marketing content to ensure everything felt cohesive and true to the apps purpose.
Throughout the process, I collaborated closely with developers to refine details and make sure every interaction felt smooth and intentional. As the app grows, Ill continue supporting future updates, improving usability and discoverability, and listening to user feedback to shape new features that make the experience even more meaningful.
Designing Worldwise reminded me that digital products can feel human when theyre created with empathy and restraint.
